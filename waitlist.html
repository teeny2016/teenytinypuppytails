What we look for in a home

  • A loving, lifelong commitment — dachshunds can be wonderful companions for 12–16 years
  • An understanding of the breed's back-care needs (no rough jumping, healthy weight)
  • Time, patience, and affection for a smart, sometimes stubborn little dog
  • A willingness to keep in touch — we love updates and photos!
